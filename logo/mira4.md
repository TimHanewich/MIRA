# MIRA Logo Design Instructions - Concept 4

Design a logo for **MIRA** as follows:

## Primary Concept
Create a logo based on the idea of a **north star / guiding star** for investing.
The symbol should represent direction, navigation, clarity, and disciplined decision-making.
It should feel like MIRA helps guide a portfolio toward opportunity.

## Exact Visual Direction
- Build a clean, minimal **star-based symbol** as the main icon
- Do not use a cartoon star; make it sharp, geometric, and premium
- The star can be a 4-point or 8-point form, or a custom angular glint
- Integrate a subtle **upward path, rising line, or directional stroke** into the star or leading toward it
- The symbol should suggest **guidance and upward movement** without becoming too literal
- Optional: place the star within a very subtle frame, arc, or partial compass shape if it remains minimal
- Keep the icon simple enough to work well at small sizes

## Style
The logo must be:
- minimal
- modern
- premium
- geometric
- clean at small sizes
- suitable for software/app branding

It should feel:
- confident
- intelligent
- calm
- directional
- trustworthy

## Typography
Pair the symbol with the word:
**MIRA**

Typography requirements:
- all caps
- geometric sans serif
- crisp, modern, and highly legible
- medium or semi-bold weight
- slightly spaced letters

Optional:
- subtly customize the **A** with a clean apex shape
- keep it fully readable

## Color
Preferred colors:
- primary: **deep navy / midnight blue**
- neutrals: **slate blue, steel blue, cool gray**
- accent: **cyan, teal, or soft silver**

Recommended treatment:
- main symbol and wordmark in dark navy
- star or highlight detail in cyan, teal, or silver

Also make sure the logo works in:
- full color
- solid black
- solid white

## Avoid
Do **not** use:
- dollar signs
- candlestick charts as the main symbol
- bulls or bears
- rockets
- coins
- cartoon stars
- crypto-style graphics
- ornate compass details

## Deliverables
Please create:
1. **Horizontal version**: symbol on left, MIRA on right
2. **Stacked version**: symbol above, MIRA below
3. **Icon-only version**: just the symbol, usable as an app icon

## Priority Direction
If only one version is explored first, use this:

**A sharp geometric north-star style icon with a subtle upward path or directional stroke integrated into it.**
