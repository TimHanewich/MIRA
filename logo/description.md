# MIRA Logo Design Instructions - Concept 2

Design a logo for **MIRA** as follows:

## Primary Concept
Create a **minimal monogram logo** built around the letter **M**.
The M should feel like:
- two rising peaks
- a market chart trend
- precision and forward momentum

The symbol should communicate intelligence, structure, and upward direction without looking like a literal stock chart.

## Exact Visual Direction
- Build a custom **abstract M** using sharp, clean geometric lines
- The left and right peaks of the M should feel like **two upward market moves**
- Keep the center valley narrow and clean
- Add a **small star, spark, or dot** near the top-right peak to represent signal detection or insight
- The symbol should be balanced and simple enough to work as an app icon
- The M should be recognizable, but stylized enough to feel like a proprietary brand mark

## Style
The logo must be:
- minimal
- modern
- premium
- geometric
- clean at small sizes
- suitable for software/app branding

It should feel:
- intelligent
- calm
- strategic
- trustworthy
- slightly futuristic

## Typography
Pair the symbol with the word:
**MIRA**

Typography requirements:
- all caps
- geometric sans serif
- crisp, modern, and highly legible
- medium or semi-bold weight
- slightly spaced letters

Optional:
- subtly customize the **A** so it has a triangular or apex-like form
- keep it fully readable

## Color
Preferred colors:
- primary: **deep navy / midnight blue**
- neutrals: **slate blue, steel blue, cool gray**
- accent: **cyan or teal**

Recommended treatment:
- main symbol and wordmark in dark navy
- star/signal point in cyan or teal

Also make sure the logo works in:
- full color
- solid black
- solid white

## Avoid
Do **not** use:
- dollar signs
- candlestick charts as the main symbol
- bulls or bears
- rockets
- coins
- cartoon robots
- flashy crypto-style graphics
- overly complex detail

## Deliverables
Please create:
1. **Horizontal version**: symbol on left, MIRA on right
2. **Stacked version**: symbol above, MIRA below
3. **Icon-only version**: just the symbol, usable as an app icon

## Priority Direction
If only one version is explored first, use this:

**A custom geometric M made from two rising peaks, with a small star or signal point near the upper-right peak.**
