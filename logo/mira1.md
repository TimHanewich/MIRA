# MIRA Logo Design Instructions

Design a logo for **MIRA** as follows:

## Primary Concept
Create a **minimal, geometric logo mark** that combines these three elements into one symbol:
1. an **abstract eye or observatory lens** shape
2. a **rising market line** inside the shape
3. a small **star, spark, or light point** near the upper-right to represent insight

The logo should look like an intelligent system spotting opportunity.

## Exact Visual Direction
- Use a clean **almond/lens silhouette** as the outer shape
- Inside it, place a **simple upward-slanting line** running from lower-left to upper-right
- The line can be straight or slightly stepped, but keep it simple
- Place a **small star, glint, or dot** at or near the upper-right end of the line
- Keep the eye/lens shape **abstract**; do **not** make it look like a literal human eye
- If desired, add a very subtle circular center/lens element, but only if it stays minimal

## Style
The logo must be:
- minimal
- modern
- premium
- geometric
- clean at small sizes
- suitable for software/app branding

It should feel:
- intelligent
- calm
- precise
- trustworthy
- slightly futuristic

## Typography
Pair the symbol with the word:
**MIRA**

Typography requirements:
- all caps
- geometric sans serif
- crisp, modern, and highly legible
- medium or semi-bold weight
- slightly spaced letters

Optional:
- subtly customize the **A** so it has a triangular or apex-like form
- keep it fully readable

## Color
Preferred colors:
- primary: **deep navy / midnight blue**
- secondary neutrals: **slate blue, steel blue, cool gray**
- accent: **cyan or teal**

Recommended treatment:
- main logo in dark navy
- star/signal point in cyan or teal

Also make sure the logo works in:
- full color
- solid black
- solid white

## Avoid
Do **not** use:
- dollar signs
- candlestick charts as the main symbol
- bulls or bears
- rockets
- coins
- cartoon robots
- flashy crypto-style graphics
- overly complex detail

## Deliverables
Please create:
1. **Horizontal version**: symbol on left, MIRA on right
2. **Stacked version**: symbol above, MIRA below
3. **Icon-only version**: just the symbol, usable as an app icon

## Priority Direction
If only one concept is explored first, use this:

**Abstract observatory-eye logo with a rising line inside and a small star/signal point at the upper-right.**
