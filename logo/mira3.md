# MIRA Logo Design Instructions - Concept 3

Design a logo for **MIRA** as follows:

## Primary Concept
Create a **circular signal/lens logo** that represents market scanning, analysis, and target identification.
The logo should feel like a smart system locking onto a high-value signal.

## Exact Visual Direction
- Use a clean **circle or rounded lens** as the main outer shape
- Inside the circle, include a **single rising diagonal line** from lower-left to upper-right
- Add one small **target point, glowing dot, or star** where the line ends in the upper-right area
- Use one or two subtle internal cuts, rings, or arcs to make the symbol feel like a scanner, lens, or radar interface
- Keep the internal shapes simple and minimal
- The final symbol should feel like a blend of **lens + signal + market movement**
- It should not look like a military reticle or a literal radar screen; keep it elegant and premium

## Style
The logo must be:
- minimal
- modern
- premium tech
- geometric
- clean at small sizes
- suitable for software/app branding

It should feel:
- analytical
- focused
- calm
- precise
- intelligent

## Typography
Pair the symbol with the word:
**MIRA**

Typography requirements:
- all caps
- geometric sans serif
- crisp, modern, and highly legible
- medium or semi-bold weight
- slightly spaced letters

Optional:
- subtly customize the **A** with a clean apex shape
- keep it fully readable

## Color
Preferred colors:
- primary: **deep navy / midnight blue**
- neutrals: **slate blue, steel blue, cool gray**
- accent: **cyan or teal**

Recommended treatment:
- circular lens in dark navy
- signal point in cyan or teal
- optional subtle gradient only if very restrained and premium

Also make sure the logo works in:
- full color
- solid black
- solid white

## Avoid
Do **not** use:
- dollar signs
- candlestick charts as the main symbol
- bulls or bears
- rockets
- coins
- cartoon robots
- crypto-style neon effects
- excessive rings or interface clutter

## Deliverables
Please create:
1. **Horizontal version**: symbol on left, MIRA on right
2. **Stacked version**: symbol above, MIRA below
3. **Icon-only version**: just the symbol, usable as an app icon

## Priority Direction
If only one version is explored first, use this:

**A premium circular lens symbol with a rising diagonal signal line and a small target point at the upper-right.**
